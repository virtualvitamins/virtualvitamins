'LUCID'

Alyssa Castaneda / Jason Manzon / Derek Kwok
ALSSYON-D

Our dreams can be entertaining, adventurous, frightening, and sometimes unrecognizable, where shape and form or reality can be bended. Whether we enjoy the escape in our dreams or fear it, there is something interesting that can be found in them. Ranging from regular day dreams, lucid, surreal, or ‘normal’ dreams, LUCID is a series of pieces that take our documented dreams and are expressed on the canvas through digital mediums.
